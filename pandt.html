4. How we use your information

  1. Create and publish content – We edit recordings for length, clarity and legal compliance, apply filters where needed, and publish them anonymously across podcast platforms, social media and future media formats.
  2. Notify you – If you provided an email, we send a single notice when your episode goes live.
  3. Visualise the journey – Postcodes create a pin-map showing where the recorder has travelled.
  4. Analytics & security – We analyse usage to maintain and secure the Service.

We never sell or rent your personal information.

7. Your privacy rights

Under Australian Privacy Principles and, where applicable, the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access – Request a copy of your personal information we hold
  • Correction – Request we correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion – Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Portability – Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object – Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Restrict – Request we limit processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent – Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise these rights, email privacy@sekretspodcast.com with proof of identity. We will respond within 30 days (or 1 month under GDPR).

8. Security measures

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for audio files and personal information
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorised personnel only
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Incident response procedures for potential breaches
  • Secure deletion protocols for expired data

In the event of a data breach that poses serious harm, we will notify affected individuals and the OAIC within 72 hours as required by the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.
